Campus Life



School Library

Was established in 2010, D.P.S. Indirapuram offers a state of the art library

Pursuing excellence, the school has provided a spacious Air Conditioned Library with more than 30,000 books which enable children to explore and glean information. A Resource Centre for Teachers has also been set up to help teachers update their skills.





School Auditorium

Was established in 2012, D.P.S. Indirapuram offers two state of the art auditoriums

The school has an aesthetically planned huge air-conditioned multi-purpose auditorium for recreational and educational activities. It has a seating capacity of 2000 people and is equipped with state-of-the-art sound and light effects.

A multipurpose air-conditioned mini-Auditorium is used for conducting workshops, class assemblies and competitions.

Atrium

Was established in 2011, D.P.S. Indirapuram offers a multi-functional Atrium

The Atrium in the new building serves as the play area and has a basket ball court, two badminton courts and a tennis court where expert coaches in every discipline come to train students.

The school also offers a fully functional play ground that offers ample space for out door games such as cricket, foot ball and field hockey.

School Cafeteria

D.P.S. Indirapuram offers a fully functional cafeteria

The cafeteria provides healthy and balanced meals and snacks for children during school hours.













What's New @ DPS Indirapuram



  • Christ Katha celebrating Christmas

    Christmas was celebrated with much fanfare at DPS Indirapuram. The children from pre-Primary wing presented the play "Christ Katha" depicting the life of Jesus Christ.



    Children of class Nursery were taken out to watch the movie "Frozen 2" and they thoroughly enjoyed the experience over tubs of popcorn and juice.



    Children of class VI and VII decorated Christmas trees in class, created beautiful reindeer head-gear and Christmas cards.

  • Trip to Kingdom of Dreams

    The students of class V, VI and VII went for a trip to the famous "Kingdom of Dreams" in Gurgaon. The children thoroughly enjoyed the grandeur of the venue and the aesthically designed sets and locations.



    The children enjoyed the spledor of the "Culture Gully" and also watched a bollywood musical play. The children also enjoyed regional delicacies from different states.



    They returned with majestic memories for life.

  • Trip to Kidzania, Noida

    The students of class VI and VII went on an excursion to Kidzania on 31st January 2021 and 6th February 2021 respectively.



    The students at Kidzania participated in various activities which stimulated their creative thinking and boosted their self confidence. Through various activities children also learnt social skills and team work.



    It was a fun filled learning experience for them.

  • Scholar Badge Ceremony - Class VI

    The students of class VI were awarded scholar awards based on there performance in the annual exams and other co-curricular activities throughout the year.



    The principal Mrs. Sangeeta Hajela awarded the scholar badges to the meritorious students and also encouraged them to continue to work hard in academics, sports and other co-curricular activities for an all-round development.

  • New temperature controlled indoor swimming pool

    DPS Indirapuram has recently upgraded to an indoor, temperature controlled swimming pool.



    The Swimming Pool of DPS Indirapuram is a six-laned, half-olympic size pool. Regular swimming sessions are held during the school hours, evening games and in the summer vacations, in the presence of experienced coaches (lady coach for girls), a life-guard and an attendant. Regularly maintained and cleansed, it has trained many swimming champions who have won several awards and medals in various competitions all over India..



School Achievements

Inter School Group Song Competition
Won first prize in Inter School Group Song Competition (International Education Week 2019-20) in French group song category

The inter school competition was part of the International Education week celebrations and more than 40 schools from Delhi NCR participated in this event. D.P.S. Indirapuram was represented by the students on classes VI and VII who won the first prize through recitation of the famous french song Casablanca.

Inter School Dance Competition (Nrityanjali)
Won first prize in Inter School Dance Competition (Nrityanjali)

DPS Indirapuram hosted the Inter DPS Zonal Dance festival 'Nrityanjali 2019' on 2nd August 2019. The themes for the festival were - 'Mohan to Mahatma', an ode to 150 Years of Mahatma Gandhi and 'Sushakti' - Empowered women of History/Mythology. 24 Delhi Public Schools from Delhi and the NCR participated in this event, mesmerizing the gathering with their spellbinding performances.

D.P.S. Indirapuram won the prize for 'Best Choreography'and 'Best Costume'.

Ranked No. 1 in Times School Survey 2020 in Leaders category
D.P.S. Indirapuram declared the first amongst the Leaders in Ghaziabad school ranking

In a recently conducted survey spanning 750 schools of the Delhi NCR region, DPS Indirapuram grabbed the top rank for Ghaziabad region in the top rated Leaders Category.

The ranking process included a holistic research, based on both objective and perceptual study .The survey conducted was based upon parameters such as infrastructure, facilities, board results, Exchange programs, good will among top educators, parents and students, extra-curricular activities etc.

International Hindi Olympiad 2020
DPS Indirapuram received the prestigious Rashtriya Hindi Unnayak Samman 2020 for being the school from which the maximum number of students had bagged medals and trophies

The International Hindi Olympiad 2020 was conducted online by the Hindi Vikas Sansthan, Delhi on 22nd November 2020. 493 students of DPS Indirapuram participated with great enthusiasm and zeal in this Olympiad.

A total of 84 students from classes III to X were awarded gold medals, 20 were awarded silver and 5 students were awarded the bronze medal at the first level of this prestigious Olympiad.

The second level of the Olympiad was conducted on 30th December 2020. 5 students of the school were awarded the Rashtriya Hindi Pratibha Samman award and 8 students of the school were felicitated with the Hindi Bal Shreshth Samman at the second level (National level) along with gold medals and trophies.
